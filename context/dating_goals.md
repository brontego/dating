# Dating Goals

## Current posture

- reenter dating gently instead of treating it like an urgent life project
- use Hinge as a lightweight discovery tool, not as a constant background task
- optimize for actual fit and energy, not just matches or replies

## What this system should protect

- low pressure
- emotional honesty
- bounded time
- real curiosity
- basic safety and pacing

## What a good near-term result looks like

- profile feels current enough to use without embarrassment
- screening decisions feel clearer and less mood-driven
- first messages feel easier to send
- dating activity does not crowd out study, food, money, or household reboot work
