# Messaging Voice

## Target tone

- warm
- curious
- lightly playful
- specific
- not overpolished

## Opener rules

- anchor to something in the profile
- one idea is enough
- one question is usually enough
- avoid resume-interview energy
- avoid trying to impress before there is any actual rapport

## Reply rules

- move the conversation forward without writing mini-essays
- mirror the other person's depth without going flat
- keep sounding like yourself, not like a templating engine
