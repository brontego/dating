# Profile Strategy

## Main idea

The profile should stop the scroll cleanly and then convert that attention into real fit.

## Photo strategy

- first photo: clear face, relaxed expression, no ambiguity about who you are
- second or third photo: a concrete activity or environment that feels like your real life
- one photo can be a small curiosity hook, but only if it still feels authentic
- avoid loading the profile with too many similar angles or outfits

## Prompt strategy

- specific beats generic
- lightly playful beats try-hard clever
- reveal enough real texture that an opener can hook into something
- prompts should invite easy replies without sounding like job-interview prompts

## Reboot rule

- good enough beats perfect
- one clean profile pass is more valuable than endless prompt tinkering
