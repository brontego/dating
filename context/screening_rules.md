# Screening Rules

## Keep

- there is at least one clear point of curiosity
- the profile shows some effort or personality
- the life energy feels reasonably compatible
- nothing obvious conflicts with the kind of relationship pace you want

## Maybe

- attraction is there, but the profile is thin
- one detail is compelling, but the rest is still fuzzy
- the person seems interesting enough to justify one opener, not instant optimism

## Pass

- no real effort, almost no usable signal
- obvious incompatibility in tone, values, or relationship goals
- chaos, disrespect, cruelty, or heavy negativity
- something feels off in a way that would need rationalizing

## Reminder

Do not confuse attraction with fit.
Do not let tiredness or loneliness lower the screening bar.
