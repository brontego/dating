## Internal Tracking Structure

For each real person, we want to track:

1. `name`
2. `platform`
3. `stage`
4. `last meaningful interaction`
5. `next action`
6. `booked logistics` if a date exists
7. `hooks and compatibility cues`
8. `watchouts or unknowns`
9. `export status`

Use the files this way:

- `state/current_matches.yaml`: live people and date status
- `state/active_conversations.yaml`: threads that actually need response or pacing help
- `deliverables/current/conversation_export_prompt.md`: what to paste into ChatGPT web
- `deliverables/current/reply_drafts.md`: draft support when you want wording help

## Pre-Date Rules

- Do not flood the thread before the date.
- Confirm logistics only when needed.
- Pick the outfit and timing early enough that day-of stress stays low.
- Let the in-person date do the work instead of trying to manufacture chemistry in text.

## Post-Date Fields We Want

After each date, capture:

1. vibe
2. strongest point of connection
3. friction or concern
4. whether a second date should be pursued
5. what the next move is and by when
