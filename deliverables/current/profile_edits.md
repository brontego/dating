# Profile Edits

## First-pass checklist

- check first photo:
  does it look current, clear, and easy to read quickly?
- check prompt order:
  does each prompt reveal something useful or attractive?
- remove anything that sounds generic, defensive, or stale
- keep at least one prompt or photo that makes an opener easy

## Rewrite direction

- warm over polished
- specific over generic
- lightly playful over try-hard clever
- real-life texture over abstract virtues

## Good profile test

If someone paused on the profile for `20` seconds, would they have at least one easy way to message you?
