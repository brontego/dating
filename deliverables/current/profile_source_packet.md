# Profile Source Packet

Status: working draft seeded from current system notes.

Use this as the text reference for the ChatGPT dating project.
Treat anything marked `needs confirmation` as editable rather than final truth.

## Known public-facing hooks

- Lives in the Des Moines area.
- Bikes regularly and has real cycling goals.
- RAGBRAI is a real conversation hook.
- Likes reading.
- Likes farmers markets.
- Likes music.
- Likes video games.
- Coffee and bookshop dates fit well.

## Broader personal context the copilot should know

- 2026 is a real rebuild-and-build year, not just a maintenance year.
- Fitness and biking matter because they are part of a real life direction, not just a hobby badge.
- Bronson tends to do better with specifics, warmth, and real shared texture than with generic banter.
- He is thoughtful and systems-minded, but the dating voice should still feel natural.
- He is more "warm and observant" than "slick and high-volume."

## Desired voice in messages

- warm
- curious
- grounded
- specific
- lightly playful
- not overly polished
- not thirsty
- not trying to sound cooler than he is

## Likely attraction pattern

These are working hypotheses, not finished facts:

- intelligent without being performative
- kind and emotionally steady
- has some real-life curiosity
- can enjoy leaving the house and doing things
- some overlap with books, music, biking, food, or exploring
- more wholesome than chaotic

## Likely turnoffs

- almost no usable profile signal
- disrespectful or chaotic energy
- profiles that already require rationalizing
- conversations where the other person gives nothing back
- generic or performative coolness

## Conversation strengths to preserve

- noticing an actual detail
- lightly playful phrasing
- asking one solid question instead of three
- sounding like a person, not a copywriter

## Things the AI should be careful with

- Do not over-index on biking if the match gives better hooks elsewhere.
- Do not make every opener about optimization, productivity, or self-improvement.
- Do not force flirtiness too early.
- Do not write as if Bronson is trying to close a sale.

## Missing details to gather in the self-interview

- actual relationship goal and pace
- what kind of partner feels best in practice
- strongest current profile prompts and photos
- values, lifestyle, and scheduling realities
- dealbreakers and caution flags
- preferred first-date plans
- what kinds of messages feel most like him
- what he wants AI to help with most: openers, replies, screening, or all three
