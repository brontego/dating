# Reply Drafts

## Anna pre-date maintenance

- "Nice, that sounds good. Looking forward to it."
- "Perfect. Sunday at `10:30` works for me."
- "That sounds fun. I am looking forward to meeting you."

## Keep momentum

- "Okay, that is a much better answer than I was expecting. `___` is a strong detail."

## Light tease plus question

- "That sounds suspiciously well thought out. How long have you been refining that answer?"

## Shift from topic to personality

- "That tells me a lot about your taste actually. What is the version of that answer that your friends would give?"

## Move toward meeting if the vibe is good

- "You seem easy to talk to. I would be up for continuing this over coffee or a drink sometime if you are."

## Rule

Keep replies human, short, and curious.
Do not overprove wit.
Do not text like you are trying to pre-play the whole date.
