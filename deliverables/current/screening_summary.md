# Screening Summary

## Keep

- clear curiosity
- visible effort
- compatible energy
- no obvious pacing or values mismatch

## Maybe

- some attraction but thin signal
- one strong hook with not much else yet
- worth one opener, not worth instant optimism

## Pass

- almost no usable profile signal
- obvious incompatibility
- disrespect or chaos
- anything that already feels like it needs rationalizing

## Reminder

The point is not to maximize matches.
The point is to avoid tired, lonely, or impulsive decisions.
