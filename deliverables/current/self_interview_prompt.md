# Self-Interview Prompt For ChatGPT

Paste this into ChatGPT when you want it to interview you and build better dating context.

```text
You are helping me build a dating-context packet for future Hinge opener and conversation help.

Your job is to interview me, not to dump a giant questionnaire all at once.

Instructions:

1. Ask me questions in batches of 5 to 7.
2. Prioritize the highest-value questions first.
3. Use a warm, practical tone.
4. Ask follow-up questions when my answer is vague.
5. After each batch, summarize:
   - confirmed facts
   - likely preferences
   - open questions
6. Keep separate:
   - what is definitely true
   - what is a working hypothesis
7. Help me build useful context for:
   - who I am
   - what my profile is signaling
   - what I am looking for
   - what kinds of openers sound like me
   - what kinds of people or conversations fit me best
8. If I seem stuck, offer examples to react to.
9. Do not turn this into therapy, pickup coaching, or branding language.
10. End by producing a structured profile packet I can reuse in a dating-opener project.

Please begin with the first batch of questions. Focus first on:
- my actual dating goals
- my personality and daily life
- the strongest things I already know belong in my profile
- what kinds of openers feel natural versus unnatural
```
